ABOUT ME

Developer || Designer || Gamer

My name is Lucian Pauna and I am a front-end developer. My main focus is building responsive and interactive websites from scratch using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. To speed up the process, I use tools such as Gulp, PostCSS and NPM. Because I believe the key for any successful project is comunication, I like to share updates periodically with my clients until the finalization of the project, and hopefully after.

There is nothing I enjoy more than writing code from scratch. My journey as a developer started in December 2017 after writing my first line of code ”Hello World!”. Shortly after, coding became my passion, and since then, I have been spending my time learning something new every day. My goal is each day to get better than the previous day.

When I’m not writing code, I am probably somewhere around the world with my girlfriend, discovering new amazing places and awesome people... or just at home, playing video games.
